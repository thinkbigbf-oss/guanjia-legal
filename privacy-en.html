Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026 · 中文

Kept ("the App") respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address you provide at sign-up, used for login, password recovery, and account management.
  • Items you add: item names, categories, status, photos, and notes. This data is stored locally on your device. Photos stay on your device.
  • The App does not collect your contacts, location, or device identifiers for tracking, and does not use your data for advertising.

2. How we use information

  • To provide core features: home inventory, idle-item reminders, and decluttering suggestions.
  • AI features: when you use AI features such as the daily "Butler" brief or photo recognition, the relevant item text (or image) is forwarded through our server to third-party AI services (DeepSeek and Anthropic) to generate a result. This content is used only to produce the immediate result and is not used for any other purpose.

3. Storage and third-party services

  • Account and authentication are provided by Supabase.
  • Email delivery (verification codes, password recovery) is provided by Resend.
  • AI processing is provided by DeepSeek and Anthropic. Note that these providers may process data on servers located outside your country.
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS, and we take reasonable measures to protect it.

4. Information sharing

Other than the service providers listed above that are necessary to deliver the App's features, we do not sell or share your personal information with any third party.

5. Your rights

  • Access & export: you can view and export your item data within the App.
  • Delete your account: go to Me → Account → Delete account in the App, or email us at the address below. We will delete your account and associated data within 3 business days.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated; changes will be posted on this page. Significant changes will be noted in the App.

8. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions, contact support@douhouse.com
